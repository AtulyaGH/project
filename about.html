About Us

In 1999, ICY-HOT opened the doors of it's very first store. A store with a mission. A mission to create, natural food, avoiding the obscure chemicals, additives & preservatives common to so much of the 'prepaared' and 'fast' food available in the market today.

Naturally Authentic

We use the most healthy and natural procedures to process our creams.You won't find 'use by' dates on our fresh products. Our ingredients are 100% organic. We offer our unsold food to charity at the end of each day rather than keeping it to sell the next day.

To Your Door

We know that not everyone is able to reach the stores to enjoy our delicious products. So, we have a very fast delivery system that can get to you within an hour even in the remotest are of the city. Orders are vaccum packed & kept inside a mobile refrigerator hence keeps your order fresh & frozen.
